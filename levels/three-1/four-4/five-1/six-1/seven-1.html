• Home

Mr. Goose attempts to chase you to the back of the room, but accidentally steps in the center of the carpet. The circular design begins to glow as the floorboards underneath Mr. Goose immediately disappear. You hear a comically long "HOOOOOOOOOOONK" before Mr. Goose hits the ground. The book spins and glows a green color, pointing behind you at the wide open front door of the mansion. You grab the book and race outside into a field full of tall blades of grass. The book helps guide you home, and for the first time, glows a happy yellow color.

Congratulations! You survived and escaped. You made a new friend along the way.
